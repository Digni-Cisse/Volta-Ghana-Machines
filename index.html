Heavy Machinery in Ghana: Frequency Asked Questions

Can I rent equipment for a 3-month project?
Yes, you can rent equipment for a 3-month project. Volta Machinery offers flexible rental terms across the entire fleet of excavators, loaders, MEWPs and more.
What happens if a machine breaks down on-site?
On machine breakdown, Volta technicians are dispatched immediately with genuine spare parts. We get you back to work within hours, not days.
Are Engirise machines easy to maintain?
Yes, Engirise machines are easy to maintain. Their designs prioritize "serviceability" - meaning simple access points for daily checks and robust mechanical systems.
Do you deliver outside Accra?
Yes, Volta Machinery serves all 16 regions of Ghana. We also have the logistics capability to support projects across the wider West African borders.
Are your machines built for West African conditions?
Yes, all Engirise machines are ‘tropicalized’ to handle West African conditions. They have enhanced cooling systems, filters, and configurations for high heat, dust, and humidity.
